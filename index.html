Dedication

Mission

Our mission is to meet the basic needs of the most vulnerable groups in society, to empower them, and to protect their rights.

Vision

Our vision is to see a society where the basic needs of all people are met, where the vulnerable are empowered, and where human rights are protected.

Our Objectives

We work across multiple thematic areas to create sustainable change in communities.

1. Meeting Basic Needs

Providing food, clean water, solar lighting, shelter, and health services to communities in need.

2. Education Access

Helping children and youth return to school and access quality education.

3. Economic Empowerment

Supporting income through vocational training, small loans, and job projects.

4. Health Care

Expanding health facilities and awareness on disease prevention.

5. Rights Protection

Safeguarding women and children against violence and discrimination.

6. Emergency Support

Quick response to natural or man-made disasters.

7. Youth Capacity

Providing vocational training, loans, and job creation for youth.

8. Disabilities Support

Assisting people who have become disabled for various reasons.

9. Access to Justice

Strengthening the justice system and supporting inmates.
